Restock Bottom

Stocking

Creamy horseradish pre-portioned into 2oz cups in the walk-in. Take what you need in order to have 10 in the station.

Raw horseradish is located in the walk-in, on the top shelf, in a large plastic jar.

You'll need to portion into 2oz portion cups with a lid.

You may want to wear gloves and use a spoon for this.

Mayonnaise can be found in the walk-in. It is sent in bags that are cut open and placed into a plastic container.

The exact location of these can vary, so keep your eyes peeled!

If you cannot find it in the walk-in, you may open a new pack, which can be found in the boxes under the tables on the back line.

You'll need to portion into 2oz portion cups with a lid.

You may want to wear gloves and use a spoon for this.

Sour cream is located in the walk-in. It will be on the bottom shelf of the green rack at the end of the walk-in.

You will carefully open the container using a knife.
(The large kitchen knives are available but very sharp. You must be conscious of those around you and clean it off when you are done; DO NOT put it into dish)

You may empty the entirety of a sour cream bag into a plastic 6 pan (small, square, deep) and place a lid on the container.

Pickle spears are located in the walk-in, in large 5 gallon white buckets (read the label to verify!).

Breaking the seal on these can be challenging so if you need help, ask!

Using a glove, fill the pickle spear 3rd pan from restock bottom up with pickles, ensuring there is sufficient pickle juice in the pan.

Lemons are located in the walk-in, middle shelf, middle of the fridge.

They will be cut and portioned into a 3rd pan for you, so all you need to do is bring one up!

Creole mustard is pre-portioned into 3rd pans in the walk-in (middle shelf, middle of the fridge).

Simply grab a new container and merge them if the one in restock is low.

Red onions are located in the walk-in, just about straight ahead upon entry.

They will be pre-diced and portioned into 6 pans, so all you need to do is grab one if restock bottom is low.

Whipped butter is located in the walk-in, top shelf, towards the back of the fridge.

Cheddar is located in the walk-in, middle shelf, middle of the fridge.

We have two different grades of cheddar, one large and one small. Make sure you are grabbing the thicker grade.

Remove any expired labels (including any with today's date)

Make your way to the back line where the label maker is and print out new labels for any expired labels in the station

If the label maker is logged out, the credentials are 'user' and 'password'.


Cleaning

Using a sanitizing spray and a clean towel, wipe off the doors and handles.

Inside the doors of the station are rubber gaskets. They have creases in them that will collect junk. Take care to thoroughly clean these.

Clean the inside of the station thoroughly. This includes shelves and ledges inside the station.

If you weren't careful to be clean when portioning in the earlier steps, make sure you clean up your mess.

This includes spare portion cups and lids, dirty knives, and any food product that may have missed the container.
