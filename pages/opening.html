Opening

  • Turn the lever on the drain pipe underneath to block the drains
  • Set up all the metal containers.
  • Lift one of the pans to fill the station with water until it is touching the deep metal pans.
  • Turn the expo station dial to 7.
  • Using the guide below, fill the metal pans with:
    • Lemon pepper butter
    • Garlic butter
    • 1/2 a block of butter
  • The remaining sauces will be given to you from hot prep through the window
  • Flip over the bus pan under the station.
  • Fill the red soap buckets under the stations with sanitizer solution and place them under the stations.

  • Take off the station lid and slide it into its storage space in broil (above the meat display)
  • Remove any plastic lids and give them to dish.
  • Fill the 3 small rectangular metal pans with:
    • Pickles
    • Diced red onions
    • Lemons
  • Ensure that everything in the station is full and that restock bottom is fully stocked.
  • Using a green cutting board, gloves, and a sharp knife, chop parsley and put it into a 3.25oz portion cup. (it may need time to dry in the window!)
  • Fill a 3.25oz portion cup with grated parmesan.
  • Place the parsley and parmesan into the shakers and snap on a yellow shaker lid.

  • Put on nozzles (clear first, then black)
  • Brew all four teas, making sure the tea nozzles are on and secure.
  • Add some ice to the metal 3rd pan at each soda station and place a container of lemons into them.
  • Brew regular and decaff coffee
  • Put away any bar glasses that may remain from the previous night.
  • Using the bags hanging on the wall by dry storage, portion sugar for tea:
    • Sugar can be found in hot prep
    • 6 cups (8oz, so squat cups work) per bag
    • Monday-Thursday = 6 bags
    • Friday-Sunday = 10 bags
    • Double check everything is stocked (ice, cups, lids, straws, etc)

  • Place some ice into a large hotel pan and place these sauces in it with a ladle:
    • Honey Mustard
    • Ranch
    • Blossom Sauce
