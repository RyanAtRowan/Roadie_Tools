Cleaning

This includes the computer screen, cables, and printer.

Tie up the trash bag and take it out to the dumpster. You can find new trashbags in the hose area within dish.

Be sure to clean the trash can cabinet while you have it out!

This includes the top counter, shelves, ledges, and front of the station

It is the hosts reponsibility to fill the bin.

However, if it is empty, you need to clean out any peanut shells and dust.

Booster seats, in-booth highchairs, and highchairs by your POS need to be wiped clean.

You responsibility is to generously sweep in front of the station.

Stocking

Large (9in x 9in) boxes are located above to-go, or on the rack above the doorway near the bathrooms.

Fully stock the napkins on top of the station, and get an extra pack for in the station.

Squat cups can be found above the drop machine. (Lately, the kitchen has been taking the box into the back, you can ask your expo or point line-worker for a sleeve)

Squat cup lids are located in the corral, middle shelf of the green rack.

Stock the portion cup lid bin with 2oz and 3.25oz portion cup lids.

Lids can be found above the to-go table on the rack.

The bin with sugars and wipes has 3 caddys (one of each: Sugar, Sweet & Low, Splenda) and wet napkin wipes. These will all need to be stocked.

Sugars and wet napkin wipes are usually by condiments.

Our POS printers use thermal paper. This is the single ply, glossy paper located in the condiments cabinet.

Stock your station fully with bread plates (3 stacks).
