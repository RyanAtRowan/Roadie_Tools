Cleaning

Assuming soda 1 has already cleaned their teas, you must clean the remaining dispensers.

Please make sure that you are leaving a pitcher of each tea for your coworkers if there are still guests in the building.

Each soda and tea dispenser has nozzles that need to be soaked in boiling water for 5 minutes.
(Run hot water from the coffee machine into a metal 6 or 3rd pan)

Take each nozzle apart:
Soda stations: You can twist the black component and pull down, and pull out the white component.
  Both of these for each station needs to be soaked. Tea Dispensers: You can unscrew the lever component and then pull out the rubber gasket.
  All three of these components need to be soaked.

Once the station has been fully cleaned, you may dump and organize the nozzles into the soda station rack. Please take care to leave all components completely broken down so they will fully dry.

Soda stations have three major drain components, they must all be cleaned.

The rack: This metal rack is what you set your drinks on.

The backsplash: This is the metal wall that keeps fountain drinks from going into the machine.

The drain: Finally, the plastic drain can be lifted out of the station.

Be sure to clean inside of the machine while the drain is removed, and replace all components into the station.

The tea brewing machine must be wiped and cleaned (including under it)

Take care to empty and clean the brew basket and wipe the inside of the machine.

Coffee pots must be emptied and cleaned, inside and out.

Take care to empty and clean the brew basket and wipe the inside of machine.

You will need to fully wipe down the machine (including under it)

Replace any syrups that are low and wipe down each of them.

You can wrap each nozzle on the syrups using a single sheet of foil. (tear it to size and simply cover the hole!)
Grenadine has a built in cap, so no need to wrap it!

Fully remove the rack from the wall and ensure that the rack and the wall behind it are wiped clean.

You must clean the walls and wipe the countertops around your responsible stations (soda station, coffee and tea brewing stations).
