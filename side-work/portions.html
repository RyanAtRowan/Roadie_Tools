Honey Mustard & Ranch

  • Get a pan of ranch and honey mustard from the walk-in.
  • Get some small and large portion cups and their lids
  • (You may find it easier to portion with a ladel!)
  • Portion enough large/small honey mustard and ranch to fill up the expo (like seen below)
  • Portion Honey Mustard and Ranch into deep metal "third pans" as follows:
  • Monday-Thursday:
    • 1/2 pan of large Honey Mustard
    • 1/2 pan of small Honey Mustard
    • 2 pans of large Ranch
    • 2 pans of small Ranch
  • Friday-Sunday:
    • 1 pan of large Honey Mustard
    • 1 pan of small Honey Mustard
    • 3 pans of large Ranch
    • 3 pans of small Ranch
  • These pars are what you need in total.
    For example: if there is already 1 small Ranch portioned on Saturday, you will only need to portion 2 small Ranch pans
