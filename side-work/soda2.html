Soda Station 2

  • Turn off the coffee maker (if your fellow servers do not actively need it)
  • Empty the coffee pots and clean using hot water.
  • Take out drip and discard any filter and/or grinds inside.
  • Clean the coffee drip using hot water


  • Put a few lemons into a cup or monkey bowl if the restuarant is still open.
  • Put a plastic lid on the lemon container and put it into the walk-in. (make sure its labeled!)
  • Empty the ice from the lemon container and fill it with hot water.
  • Put the knife and tongs into the hot water bath.


  • Clean the remaining two tea dispensers.
  • If your fellow servers still need tea, dump the tea into large pitchers (found on the back line). Otherwise, dump them into the dish strainer behind the wall
  • Remove the dispenser nozzles and put them into the hot water bath you created.
  • Use hot water to clean the tea dispensers, inside and out!
  • Go to the tea brewing station and remove the dripper from the top.
  • Empty out any filter and/or grinds that may be there.
  • Clean the drip using hot water.
  • Use hot water to clean the tea station, getting rid of any sugar or tea from the counter and walls.
  • Place your clean tea dispensers into the clean tea brewing station.


  • Fill the ice up.
  • Remove employee cups from the soda station 2, and wipe down the counter
  • Make sure you wipe down under the coffee machine and in the syrup shelf.
  • Wipe down the syrups and wrap the syrup pump nozzles
    • The pump nozzles only need the small hole covered, just use ONE sheet of foil torn into 4 pieces please.


  • Take off the nozzles (both clean and black) from the soda machine (gentle, they come off easy) and put them into the hot water to soak.
  • take apart the soda station grate:
    • Remove the metal grate
    • The back wall behind the soda levers should lift up, and out of the station.
    • Remove the plastic bottom that leads to the drain.
    • Clean these three components very well, they can get very sticky.
    • While you have the plastic component removed, you can clean under it. (This can get gross fast, so don't skip this!!)
    • Return the components in order.. plastic first (make sure drain is aligned with hole), metal back wall second, and metal grate third
    • The back wall should slot up into the top, make sure it is properly seated or else it could get stuck!


  • Wipe off the station. Soda levers included!
  • Stock up kids cups, employee cups, lids, and straws.
  • Make sure you have a knife and tongs, and two clean metal third pans.
  • Bring up and put away any glasses that dish may have for you.
  • Dump your nozzles into the station grate, and leave them off. Even the tea nozzles. To prevent mold, we leave them off to dry over-night.
  • Your station should look like the picture above. (Except with the tea nozzles off and on the grate)
